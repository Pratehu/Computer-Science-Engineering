>Halfpace
It is also known as "half-pace" or "half-landing," is an architectural term used to describe a small, intermediate landing or platform located halfway up a flight of stairs. This landing is typically positioned between two full flights of stairs, allowing individuals to pause, change direction, or access a different part of a building. Halfpaces are often found in buildings with grand or elaborate staircases, such as historical homes, palaces, and large public buildings
