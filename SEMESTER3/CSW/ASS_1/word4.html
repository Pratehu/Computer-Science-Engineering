>Kakorrhaphiophobia
It is an uncommon and specific phobia that is characterized by an intense and irrational fear of failure or defeat. This fear can be debilitating, leading individuals to avoid situations or tasks where they perceive a risk of failing. The term "kakorrhaphiophobia" is derived from the Greek words "kakos," meaning "bad," "rhaphio," meaning "seam," and "phobos," meaning "fear." Essentially, it encapsulates a fear of falling apart or breaking down under pressure.
